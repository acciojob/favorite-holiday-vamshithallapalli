Paris, France

Paris, the capital city of France, is renowned for its art, architecture, and culinary delights. It is often called the "City of Light" and is one of the most visited tourist destinations in the world.


Top 5 Attractions

  1. Eiffel Tower – The iconic iron structure offers breathtaking views of the city.
  2. Louvre Museum – Home to thousands of works of art, including the Mona Lisa.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – A masterpiece of Gothic architecture that stands on the Île de la Cité.
  4. Champs-Élysées – A famous avenue perfect for luxury shopping and people-watching.
  5. Montmartre – A charming hilltop neighborhood known for its artistic vibe and stunning views.

Must-Try Foods

  • Croissants – These flaky pastries are a breakfast essential.
  • Macarons – Colorful and delicate, these are perfect sweet treats.
  • Escargots – Snails prepared with garlic butter, a French delicacy.
  • Ratatouille – A hearty vegetable dish that's full of flavor.
  • Crepes – Thin pancakes served with sweet or savory fillings.
